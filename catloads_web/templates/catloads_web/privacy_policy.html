  • Introduction

Catloads is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

  • Information We Collect

We collect the following information from users:

  • Contact Information: Name, Email address, Phone number (optional), Country.
  • Payment Information: We do not collect or store any credit card or bank details. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway, Razorpay.
  • Browsing Data: We collect information about your browsing activity on our website, such as pages viewed and products searched for.
  • How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing Orders and Delivering Products: We use your contact information to process your orders, deliver your digital products, and provide download instructions and tutorials if applicable. We may also store your purchase history for record-keeping purposes.
  • Sending Marketing Communications (with opt-out options): We may use your email address to send you marketing communications about our products and services. You can always unsubscribe from these emails by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any marketing email.
  • Improving Your Website and Product Offerings: We use browsing data to understand your interests and preferences and improve our website and product offerings.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. This information is anonymized and does not identify individual users.
  • Information Sharing

We share your information with the following third-party service providers:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay is our secure payment gateway that processes your payments.
  • Analytics Providers: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic.

We only share the minimum amount of information necessary with these providers and have contractual agreements with them to ensure they protect your data according to our standards and relevant privacy regulations.

  • Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. We use the following measures to protect your information:

  • Secure Servers: Our website is hosted on secure servers with firewalls and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Encryption: We encrypt sensitive information in transit (using HTTPS)
  • Data Access Restrictions: We limit access to customer data within our organization on a "need-to-know" basis.
  • Strong Password Policies: We enforce strong password policies for all user accounts.
  • Customer Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You have the right to access your personal information.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request that we delete your personal information (subject to applicable laws).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

  • Children's Privacy

Catloads is committed to protecting the privacy of children. We comply with COPPA and other applicable laws.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us. 

We may collect information from children to provide age-appropriate content, process orders, and improve our website. We take security measures to protect children's data.

We do not share children's personal information with any third-party providers.

We encourage parents to review this policy with their children and supervise their online activities.

  • Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

  • Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Refund Policy
  • Introduction

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally do not offer refunds. However, we will consider exceptions on a case-by-case basis under specific circumstances we outline (explained below).

  • Refundable Products

We will only provide refunds for products we explicitly list as refundable. For example, if we have a guide for sale with a money-back guarantee, we will offer a 100% refund if the guide demonstrably does not function as described. In such cases, customers will need to submit the required information to prove the guide is not working after following the instructions.

  • How to Request a Refund

If we offer a refund for a specific product you purchased and you believe you meet the criteria, please contact us at [your email address] within 90 days of your purchase. Include your order number and a detailed explanation of why you are requesting a refund, along with any necessary supporting evidence (as outlined in point 2).

  • Refund Process

We will review your request and respond to you within 2  business days. If your refund is approved, we will process the refund using the original payment method.

Please note:

  • This policy applies to all purchases made on the Catloads website.
  • We reserve the right to deny refunds if we believe the request is fraudulent or abusive.
